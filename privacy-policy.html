01

Overview

Echoform is an independent acoustic design information and inquiry platform. The website provides educational resources, room-planning information, acoustic guidance, and inquiry-routing support.

This Privacy Policy describes how information may be collected, used, stored, disclosed, and protected when you interact with Echoform. It also explains the choices and rights that may be available to you under applicable privacy law.

Platform Context

Echoform is an independent acoustic design information and inquiry platform. We provide educational guidance, planning resources, and request-routing support. We do not directly perform every installation or construction service described. Where appropriate, inquiries may be connected with independent specialists. Availability, scope, pricing, materials, and outcomes vary by room, location, and provider.

03

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit a contact or acoustic-planning inquiry, the information may include:

  • Your full name.
  • Your email address.
  • The project or inquiry type you select.
  • The room type or primary acoustic goal you select.
  • Room dimensions, layout information, equipment details, photographs, sketches, acoustic concerns, visual preferences, budget context, location context, and practical restrictions that you choose to describe.
  • Advertising, collaboration, campaign, partnership, or business-proposal information.
  • Any other information you voluntarily include in a message.
  • Your confirmation that you agree to the processing described at the point of submission.

Technical and Usage Information

When you use the website, limited technical information may be generated automatically by hosting, security, analytics, or delivery systems. This may include:

  • Internet Protocol address.
  • Browser type, operating system, device category, and language.
  • Requested pages, referring page, date, time, and approximate session information.
  • Security, error, performance, and server-log information.
  • Cookie-consent choices stored in your browser.

Information We Do Not Request

Do not submit passwords, payment-card details, banking credentials, identification documents, health information, biometric information, precise private-location information, or other highly sensitive personal data through the website forms.

04

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

  • Receive, review, organize, and respond to inquiries.
  • Understand the room, acoustic concern, project type, and requested planning direction.
  • Ask for additional measurements, photographs, construction context, or other clarification.
  • Provide educational information, planning resources, or suitable next-step suggestions.
  • Assess whether an inquiry may be appropriate for an independent specialist or provider.
  • Route or introduce an inquiry where the user has requested or reasonably expects that type of assistance.
  • Evaluate advertising, collaboration, sponsorship, educational, or commercial proposals.
  • Operate, secure, troubleshoot, maintain, and improve the website.
  • Prevent spam, abuse, automated submissions, fraud, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect legal rights.
  • Maintain appropriate business and correspondence records.

Information submitted for one purpose is not intentionally used for an unrelated purpose without an appropriate reason or additional notice where required.

05

Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, information may be processed on one or more of the following grounds:

Requested Steps

Processing may be necessary to review an inquiry, respond to a request, or take steps requested before a possible agreement.

Legitimate Interests

Processing may support reasonable website operation, inquiry handling, service improvement, security, record keeping, and relevant business communication.

Consent

Consent may be relied on where you actively choose to submit information, accept optional storage, or agree to another clearly described activity.

Legal Obligations

Processing may be necessary to comply with applicable law, lawful requests, accounting requirements, dispute handling, or legal claims.

06

Sharing and Service Providers

Information may be disclosed only where reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy. Recipients may include:

  • Website hosting, infrastructure, content-delivery, security, backup, and technical-support providers.
  • Email, communication, form-processing, and business-operations providers.
  • Analytics providers where analytics are enabled and permitted by your choices and applicable law.
  • Professional advisers such as legal, accounting, compliance, or insurance advisers.
  • Independent acoustic, installation, building, studio, interior, or technology specialists where inquiry routing is appropriate.
  • Advertising, collaboration, educational, or commercial partners when necessary to evaluate or carry out a relevant proposal.
  • Authorities, courts, regulators, or other parties where disclosure is legally required or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security.
  • A successor or potential successor in connection with a merger, restructuring, sale, financing, or transfer of relevant business assets, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Service providers are expected to handle information only for authorized purposes and subject to appropriate contractual, technical, or organizational controls where required.

07

Inquiry Routing and Independent Specialists

Echoform does not directly perform every installation, construction, measurement, product-supply, or specialist service described on the website.

Where appropriate, an inquiry may be reviewed for possible connection with an independent specialist. Before information is shared, the amount of data should be limited to what is reasonably relevant to the requested project or introduction.

An independent specialist may contact you directly and may become a separate controller of information it receives. Its privacy policy, communication practices, availability, pricing, scope, and contractual terms are independent from Echoform.

No Automatic Provider Agreement

Submitting information does not guarantee that an inquiry will be routed, accepted, priced, scheduled, or fulfilled by any specialist.

Cookies and Browser Storage

The website may use cookies or similar browser-storage technologies for limited operational purposes.

Essential Storage

Essential storage may support security, form operation, navigation, accessibility, preference retention, and other functions necessary for the website to work.

Consent Choice

The cookie-consent component stores your accept or decline choice in local browser storage so that the notice does not need to appear on every visit.

You can reopen the cookie controls through the footer link. You may also remove stored preferences through your browser settings, although doing so may cause the notice to appear again.

09

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, including inquiry handling, follow-up communication, service improvement, security, dispute resolution, legal compliance, and record keeping.

Retention periods may vary depending on:

  • The nature and status of the inquiry.
  • Whether additional communication or specialist routing occurs.
  • Whether information is needed to document consent, prevent abuse, or resolve a dispute.
  • Applicable limitation periods, accounting rules, or legal obligations.
  • The sensitivity and volume of the information.

Information that is no longer required should be deleted, anonymized, or otherwise placed beyond ordinary use, subject to backup cycles and legal retention requirements.

10

Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss, misuse, or destruction.

Measures may include access controls, secure hosting, transport encryption where available, form validation, anti-spam controls, software maintenance, limited data access, backups, and incident-response procedures.

No website, email system, internet transmission, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information and use a trusted device and connection when submitting an inquiry.

11

International Data Transfers

Website infrastructure, hosting, communication tools, service providers, or specialists may operate in countries different from your own.

Where information is transferred internationally and applicable law requires safeguards, appropriate mechanisms may be used, such as adequacy decisions, approved contractual clauses, contractual commitments, risk assessments, or another lawful transfer basis.

The privacy and data-protection rules in a receiving country may differ from those in your location.

12

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Request confirmation of whether personal information is being processed.
  • Request access to personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of information in certain circumstances.
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Object to certain processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.
  • Request portable information in a structured format where applicable.
  • Submit a complaint to a competent privacy or data-protection authority.
  • Ask about international-transfer safeguards where applicable.

Rights are not absolute and may be limited by identity-verification requirements, legal obligations, the rights of others, dispute needs, security concerns, or lawful exemptions.

A request should clearly identify the relevant inquiry and the right you wish to exercise. Additional information may be requested only where reasonably necessary to verify identity and protect information from unauthorized disclosure.

13

Children

The website is intended for adults and is not designed to collect personal information knowingly from children.

A child should not submit an acoustic-planning, business, advertising, or collaboration inquiry without appropriate involvement from a parent or legal guardian.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information without appropriate authorization, contact Echoform so the situation can be reviewed.
